Our Story

At CafePlus+, thousands flock daily for a cherished coffee experience. Join our community in celebrating the art of coffee, one cup at a time.

Founded in 1988, CafePlus is the largest cafe chain in the country. We use the best quality home grown coffee to make our beverages.

Our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 30 successful years.

Mastermind in the Cafe

Allow us to introduce our coffee virtuoso, the "Mastermind in the Café." With a profound passion for the art of coffee, they have honed their skills through years of dedication and exploration, becoming a true maestro of the coffee craft.

From the first aromatic whiff to the last satisfying sip, every cup they create is a masterpiece. With expertise in coffee bean selection, roasting techniques, and brewing methods, they orchestrate a symphony of flavors that delight coffee enthusiasts near and far.

Our Vision

Creating a welcoming café where coffee fosters connections and joy, becoming a community hub that inspires positive change.

Our Mission

Crafting exceptional coffee experiences, supporting sustainability, and nurturing a warm, inclusive space for meaningful connections.
